GTG Shipbuilding Subcontractors' Notion Email Sender

This web app, called "노션 거통고조선하청지회 페이지", allows users to send pre-filled emails using their own Gmail account, based on content stored in a connected Google Spreadsheet.

The app is used in conjunction with a Notion page and is triggered via a button that links to a Google Apps Script Web App. After logging in with Google and granting consent, the app reads the data and sends emails on behalf of the user.

No user data is collected or stored. All emails are sent through the user's Gmail account only after consent.

This app was created for internal organizational communication support.
